Frequently Asked Questions

How can I access the SandScope technology?

Our technology is open-source and freely available. You can access the hardware designs, software code, and documentation through our GitHub repository. For support with implementation, contact our technical team.

Can you help train our research team?

Yes! We offer training programs for research teams, educational institutions, and conservation organizations. Contact us to discuss custom training options for your specific needs and location.

What partnerships are you looking for?

We're interested in research collaborations, conservation partnerships, educational programs, and technology integration opportunities. We particularly welcome partnerships that can help scale our impact globally.

How do I report technical issues?

For technical support, please use the contact form above with detailed information about your issue, including device specifications, error messages, and steps to reproduce the problem.

Research & Development

Our team is actively developing and testing SandScope technology in coastal environments. We collaborate with research institutions and conservation organizations worldwide to advance marine science and turtle conservation.
